Hi! I'm Becky Keyworth

I'm a Front End Web Developer with a passion for creating clean, responsive websites.

I have always enjoyed learning about the intricacies behind how things work. This started at a macro level with reading books on astrophysics and through this, I realised the workings behind many of our everyday interactions rely on communication that we don’t understand and don’t necessarily even know is happening. This led to me wanting to understand better some of those invisibile conversations. Web development is a highly fulfilling role because you create something useful, meaningful and comprehensible out of a seemingly invisible source. The constant problem solving and ‘aha’ moment when figuring out the small typo that stopped the code working as it should, keep me enjoying the challenge of coding everyday. When I'm not coding, I can be found either stuck in a book or a game.

I look forward to working with you!
